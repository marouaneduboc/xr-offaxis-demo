XR Off-Axis Projection Demo

Load a Gaussian splat (`.ply`, `.splat`, `.ksplat`), then move your head or use manual nudges to feel the off-axis depth effect.

  1. Press Load Splat File and pick your SHARP/3DGS file.
  2. If the splat appears too large/small, adjust Splat Scale.
  3. Navigate: left-drag orbit, right-drag pan, wheel/pinch zoom.
  4. Use Inside View anytime to jump back inside the scene.
  5. Enable Face Tracking (or use manual nudge controls).
  6. Drag the pawn to move it, or switch to rotate mode to spin it.
