XR Off-Axis Projection Demo

Load a Gaussian splat or `.ply`, then move your head to feel like you're looking through a portal into another world.

  1. Press Load Gaussian Splat / PLY and pick your file.
  2. Adjust Zoom to frame the portal depth you want.
  3. Enable Face Tracking for portal parallax.
  4. Use Look buttons or drag on the scene to rotate the portal camera.
  5. Use Recenter Portal anytime to restore the window pose.
