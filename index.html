XR Off-Axis Projection Demo

Load a Gaussian splat (`.ply`, `.splat`, `.ksplat`), then move your head to feel like you're looking through a portal into another world.

  1. Press Load Splat File and pick your SHARP/3DGS file.
  2. Adjust Zoom to frame the portal depth you want.
  3. Enable Face Tracking for portal parallax.
  4. Use Recenter Portal anytime to restore the window pose.

Face tracking is off. Portal is stable.
