XR Off-Axis Projection Demo

Load a Gaussian splat (`.ply`, `.splat`, `.ksplat`), then move your head or use manual sliders to feel the off-axis depth effect.

  1. Press Load Splat File and pick your SHARP/3DGS file.
  2. If the splat appears too large/small, adjust Splat Scale.
  3. Enable Face Tracking (or use Manual X/Y/Z sliders).
  4. Drag the pawn to move it, or switch to rotate mode to spin it.

Face tracking is off. Using manual controls.
